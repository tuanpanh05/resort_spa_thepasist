# Report Guideline

All reports must follow standard templates and be saved in the appropriate subdirectories.

- **WEEKLY_REPORTS**: Status updates submitted at the end of each week.
- **SPRINT_REPORTS**: Detailed summaries at the conclusion of a sprint.
- **PROGRESS_REPORTS**: Ad-hoc reports indicating milestone completion.
- **FINAL_REPORT**: The cumulative project report upon delivery.
- **LESSONS_LEARNED**: Retrospective notes.
