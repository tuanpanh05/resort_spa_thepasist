Checkout & Payment Endpoints

GET /api/invoices/{id}

Retrieve checkout details and invoice folio information by ID.

GET /api/invoices/user/{userId}

Retrieve all checkout invoices associated with a specific customer ID.

POST /api/invoices/create

Generate a new consolidated guest folio invoice from a room booking ID.

POST /api/invoices/{id}/payment-url

Acquire VNPay payment link to process the online deposit or final checkout settlement.

GET /api/invoices/vnpay-callback

Handler route for VNPay gateway webhook/IPN secure callback processing.
